ISSUANCE OF OFFICIAL ACADEMIC DOCUMENTS

Trascript of Record

The Official Transcript of Records is a record of the courses taken and grades earned of a student, including credits transferred from other schools. This may be issued for evaluation, employment, board exam, and/or Commission on Higher Education (CHED) Certification of Authentication and Verification (CAV) purposes.

CERTIFICATES

Certificates issued by the Loyola Schools Office of the Registrar include the following:

  • Certified True Copy of Diploma
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Certificate of Grades (by Semester)
  • Certificate of Non-Inssuance of ID

CONDITION FOR ISSUANCE OF OFFICIAL ACADEMIC DOCUMENTS

Any Cavite State University - Silang Campus student and alumna may request copies of their transcipt of records or certificates at any time. Students/graduates with hold order/ liability should be cleared by the concerned office first before the request will be processed. A graduate student on a provisional status may not be issued official documents pending the submission of admission requirements.

General Procedure of Document Requests

STEP

REQUESTING PARTY

SC REGISTRAR’S OFFICE

STEP 1:
Login / Create Account

Login or Register with you cvsu.one account. Register Here


STEP 2:
Request

File a request and fill-up the necessary informations including mode of delivery & payment


STEP 3:
Confirmation and Billing

Verifies and confirm details of request.

Checks eligibility of student for issuance of official academic records

Verifies and asks for confirmation of details of request for scholastic documents.

Sends billing information and payment instructions.

STEP 4:
Payment Processing

Pays for document request according to billing information and payment instructions.

Submits a scanned copy/ screenshot of deposit slip as proof of payment.

NOTE:Please wait for payment instructions before paying for your request.


STEP 5:
Payment Verification


Verifies proof of payment

STEP 6:
Document Issuance


Sends electronic (via email) or printed copies (via WExpress Courier Service) of the requested documents to the requesting party and/ or third party recipient.

NOTE:Due to campus access restrictions, printed documents shall be released only through an accredited courier. Shipping charges apply.

Electronic documents are released only through registrar.silang@cvsu.edu.ph to the requestor's identified email address recipient.

NOTE: The CvSU Silang Registrar's Office is currently receiving an unusual large volume of requests. The process of verifying requests via online channels is taking longer than the in-person process. Average turnaround time is between 10 to 15 days.
